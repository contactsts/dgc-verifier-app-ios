MOBILE APPLICATION FOR THE VERIFICATION OF EU DCC - "Check DCC"

The application "Check DCC" can be used to verify the EU DCC, regulated by the provisions of Regulation (EU) no. 2021/953 and Regulation (EU) no. 2021/954, hereinafter referred to as the DCC.

How does the "Check DCC" application work?

The "Check DCC" application uses the camera of your mobile device to scan the QR code on the DCC.

After scanning, the application returns an answer that can be, as appropriate: Valid (GREEN) or Invalid (RED), depending on the result of verifying the following information in the DCC:

·          1. If the DCC is signed with a valid qualified electronic signature;

·          2. If the DCC has not been revoked by the issuer;

·          3. If the DCC complies with the validity criteria imposed by Romania, in accordance with the law.

If the result of the 3 checks mentioned above is correct, the DCC is Valid (GREEN color).

In order to establish whether the scanned QR code complies with the validity conditions established on the Romanian territory, the application “Check DCC” uses as reference the Coordinated Universal Time (UTC)

What information does the QR code on the DCC show?

After scanning the QR code, the following information regarding the holder of the DCC is displayed on the screen of your mobile device:

·          1. The result of the verification of the QR;

·          2. First name and last name of the holder;

·          3. Birth date of the holder;

·          4. The specific data of the DCC (testing, recovery, vaccination) of the holder.

The "Check DCC" application does not store data!

The application does not store or transmit data or information regarding the scanned DCC, in accordance with the provision of art. 10 para. (3) of EU Regulation no. 2021/953.

Requesting the presentation of an identity document!

According to the laws of Romania, the person carrying out the verification at the time of verification, may request the holder to present a document certifying identity thereof.
